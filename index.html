Spaulding Ranch Master Plan - Design Phase

The Spaulding Ranch Design Master Plan is a unique opportunity to create a one-of-a-kind park in the Boise Park System. The design approach focuses on (3) components:

1) Historic preservation, 2) Education and Interpretation and 3) Community Connections.

This planning process began with a Discovery Phase where we gained important feedback for what type of programs should be provided at Spaulding Ranch. Now, in this Design Phase, we need your input for where to locate these key programs and elements. You can help us by clicking on Design the Park to show us your ideas for Spaulding Ranch.
